Hello! I'm Yun, an editor and writer based in Toronto.

About Me

I am currently in my final year of English and Professional Writing at York University where I was previously the Senior Editor of Art in Existere: Journal of Art and Literature. I am currently the Senior Editor of Fiction submissions for Inventio, a student-run online literary magazine that publishes the work of post-secondary students.

As a writer, I have written literary analyses for Canadian short stories and graphic novels. My essay “Happiness and Beauty Outside the Canadian Dream” was nominated for the Avie Bennett Prize in Canadian Literature in 2023 by Dr. Patrascu-Kingsley. In 2023, I participated in the Professional Writing Student Association’s annual symposium Sententia, and co-wrote a creative nonfiction piece “Unbecoming of Our Age” with a fellow student.
